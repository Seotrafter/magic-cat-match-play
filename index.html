About The Game

Get your paws on this fluffy, brain-teasing magical Match-3 adventure! When magical cat Milo and his dog friend Oliver saw their guardian sucked into a magical toy world, they couldn't believe their eyes!

They need your help to find their guardian and bring her back to the real world. Prepare your finger-paws to swipe and match blocks, solve magical cat puzzles, and achieve purr-fect scores!
